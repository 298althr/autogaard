Terms & Conditions

1. Permit Validity: Tint permit is valid for the duration specified by the issuing authority (typically 1-2 years).

2. Medical Reason: Medical certificates must be from licensed medical practitioners and clearly state the condition.

3. Tint Level Compliance: Actual tint must match the level specified in the permit.

4. Processing Time: Standard 3-5 business days. Medical cases may take 5-7 days.

5. Government Fees: Police/FRSC fees are subject to change by authorities.

6. Refund Policy: Service fees non-refundable once application is submitted to authorities.

7. Installation: Tint installation is a separate service and not included in permit fees.

8. Compliance: You must carry the permit in your vehicle at all times.
